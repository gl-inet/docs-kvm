# How to capture text from the remote screen

The Text Recognition feature allows you to select an area on the remote screen to easily extract text. It is driven by Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology and was introduced in firmware v1.9.0.

With this feature, you can easily capture text from the remote screen (i.e., the controlled device) and copy it to the controlling device.

Follow the steps below to capture text from the remote screen.

1. Log in to your KVM console. Click the downward arrow to select your preferred recognition language, such as Chinese, English, or bilingual (Zh/En).

    ![text recognition 1](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/kvm/tutorials/text_recognition/1.png){class="glboxshadow"}

2. Click the **"T"** icon at the top center of the toolbar, and the remote screen will dim. 

    ![text recognition 2](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/kvm/tutorials/text_recognition/2.png){class="glboxshadow"}

3. Draw a box around the text you want to extract, as shown below.

    ![text recognition 3](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/kvm/tutorials/text_recognition/3.png){class="glboxshadow"}

4. The system will identify the text within the selected area automatically. You can then copy the recognized text as needed. 
    
    ![text recognition 4](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/kvm/tutorials/text_recognition/4.png){class="glboxshadow"}
